Privacy Policy

Franciscan Pilgrimage

Franciscan Pilgrimage is designed with your privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and never sell your information.

Information We Don't Collect

Franciscan Pilgrimage does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to If Then Dev servers. We do not track your usage, build profiles, or share data with advertisers. There are no analytics SDKs, no third-party tracking frameworks, and no advertising networks in this app.

Location Data

Franciscan Pilgrimage may request access to your device's location to help you find nearby Franciscan churches. Location access is requested only when you use the church finder feature and requires your explicit permission. Your location data is used solely on your device to calculate distances and is never transmitted to If Then Dev or any third party.

Local Data Storage

Your pilgrimage progress and preferences are stored locally on your device. This includes:

  • Sites you have visited or bookmarked
  • Your journey progress through guided chapters
  • Indulgence step completions
  • Media files you have downloaded for offline use

This data remains on your device and under your control.

iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled, your pilgrimage progress (visited sites, bookmarks, and journey progress) may sync across your Apple devices using your personal iCloud account. This sync uses Apple's CloudKit private database and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. Your data is stored in your private iCloud container and is not accessible to If Then Dev.

Content Delivery

Franciscan Pilgrimage retrieves pilgrimage content (site descriptions, prayers, church directories, and news) from Apple's CloudKit public database. These requests do not include any personal information, device identifiers, or user data. The app also references media hosted on archive.org, a public digital library. No personal data is sent with these requests.

Donations

Franciscan Pilgrimage offers optional in-app donations (tip jar) managed entirely through the App Store using Apple's StoreKit. Your payment information is handled by Apple and is never shared with or accessible to If Then Dev. We do not use any third-party payment processors or subscription management services. No purchase history is transmitted to our servers.

Children's Privacy

Franciscan Pilgrimage does not collect any personal information from users of any age. The app contains religious and educational content about St. Francis of Assisi and Franciscan history. Children should use the app under adult supervision.

Data Deletion

Since all user data is stored locally on your device and in your private iCloud account, you can delete your data at any time by deleting the app. Downloaded media files, bookmarks, and progress data will be removed from your device. iCloud data can be managed through your device's iCloud storage settings.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy or Franciscan Pilgrimage's data practices, please contact us at help@ifthendev.com.
