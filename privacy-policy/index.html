miSales Developer Sales Charts

API Credentials: miSales requires App Store Connect API credentials to fetch your sales data. Your private key is stored securely in your device's Keychain and never transmitted to our servers. Data Retention Cached sales data is stored locally until you clear the app cache or delete the app. You can clear cached data at any time from Settings.
