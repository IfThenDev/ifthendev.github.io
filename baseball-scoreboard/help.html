01 · How to Score a Game

How to Score a Game

Baseball & Softball Scoreboard keeps the board simple: big, glanceable numerals you can read one-handed from the dugout. Tap to add runs, advance the count, and move through the innings.

Runs, count & innings

  1. Tap a team's score to add a run.
  2. Tap the ball, strike, and out indicators to advance the count. Three outs flips the half-inning.
  3. The inning indicator tracks the top and bottom half automatically as outs accumulate.

Undo & edit

Tapped the wrong button? Use Undo to step back the last action, or tap a value to correct the score or count directly — no menu-diving, so you can fix a miscount before the next pitch.

Runs, Hits & Errors (R-H-E)

The classic R-H-E line is available on the board so you can track runs, hits, and errors for each team alongside the inning-by-inning score.

Top & bottom of the inning

The board shows which half-inning you're in. The visiting team bats in the top; the home team bats in the bottom. Outs roll the half over automatically.

Edit or correct a saved game

Every game is saved automatically. Open any saved game to correct the team names, score, count, inning, hits, errors, and even the date it was played, or add a note — then tap Mark as Final when it's over. Sort your saved games, or delete the ones you no longer need, from the Saved Games list. (All free.)

Share a scorecard

Tap Share to send a clean, branded score card — score, inning, and R-H-E — straight to Messages, AirDrop, or social. It works from the live board and from the game editor, and the image carries a scannable App Store code.

Tip: Your game is saved on-device automatically. If you put your phone away mid-inning, the score is exactly where you left it when you come back.

02 · Pro & Purchases

Pro & Purchases

Baseball & Softball Scoreboard is free to download and score with. Pro unlocks the premium features and removes ads. You can choose the option that fits how you use the app.

Lifetime vs Season Pass

  • Lifetime — a single, one-time purchase. Pay once and keep Pro forever, with no recurring charge.
  • Season Pass — an annual subscription. A good fit if you'd rather pay per season; it renews each year unless you cancel.

What Pro unlocks

  • Live Activity & Dynamic Island — the live score on your Lock Screen and notch.
  • Home & Lock Screen widgets — the current or final score, a glance away.
  • Ad-free — removes the ads shown in the free version.

Stadium mode, Siri, sharing a scorecard, per-game notes, and full saved-game editing are all free for everyone — Pro adds the Lock Screen/Home Screen surfaces above and removes ads.

Restoring your purchase

Already bought Pro and switching to a new device? Open Settings and tap Restore Purchases. As long as you're signed into the same Apple ID, your Pro unlock comes right back.

Refunds

Purchases and refunds are handled by Apple. Use Apple's Report a Problem page to request a refund.

03 · Live Activities, Widgets & Siri

Live Activities, Widgets & Siri

Live Activities and widgets are Pro; Siri works on the free version too. Together they keep the score in front of you without opening the app — on the Lock Screen, the Home Screen, and hands-free with Siri.

Live Activity & Dynamic Island

During a game, Baseball & Softball Scoreboard can pin the live score to your iPhone Lock Screen as a Live Activity, and to the Dynamic Island on supported iPhones. Glance down to see the score, inning, and count without unlocking — long-press the Dynamic Island for the expanded view.

  1. Unlock Pro to enable Live Activities.
  2. Make sure Live Activities are allowed in Settings → Baseball & Softball Scoreboard.
  3. Start scoring a game — the Live Activity appears on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.

Home & Lock Screen widgets

Add a widget to keep the score one glance away. While a game is live the widget shows the current score; once the game ends it shows the final.

  1. Long-press an empty area of your Home Screen (or your Lock Screen) until it enters edit mode.
  2. Tap the + (Home Screen) or a widget slot (Lock Screen).
  3. Search "Baseball & Softball Scoreboard" and pick the size you want.

Siri

Siri works on the free version. Ask Siri to "start a new game with Baseball & Softball Scoreboard," or ask "what's the score in Baseball & Softball Scoreboard?" — and keep your hands on the rail.

Tip: Live Activities and widgets are Pro features. Unlock Pro once (Lifetime) or per season (Season Pass) to turn them on. Siri and Stadium mode are free for everyone.

04 · Data, Privacy & Support

Data, Privacy & Support

Baseball & Softball Scoreboard is built to keep your data on your device. If Then Dev does not collect personal data, run analytics, or store your games on a server.

Where your games live

Your scores and saved games are stored on-device, in an App Group container shared with the widgets and Live Activity. There's no account, no login, and nothing uploaded to If Then Dev.

Ads & tracking

The free version shows ads through AppLovin MAX and its ad-network partners. Some ad data — including your device advertising identifier (IDFA) — is only used if you allow it via Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt. You can change that choice anytime in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking, or remove ads entirely by unlocking Pro. Full details are in the Privacy Policy.

What data does the app collect about me?

If Then Dev collects no personal data. Your games stay on your device. The free version's ads are served by AppLovin MAX, and the optional Pro purchase is managed by RevenueCat using an anonymous identifier. See the Privacy Policy for the specifics.

How do I remove ads?

Unlock Pro — either a one-time Lifetime purchase or an annual Season Pass — to remove ads and enable Live Activities and widgets. (Stadium mode and Siri are already free.)

Will I lose my game if I close the app mid-inning?

No. Games are saved automatically on-device, so an interrupted inning is right where you left it when you reopen the app.

How do I get support?

Reach out anytime — contact support and we'll help.
