Watch-First Pickleball Scoreboard

The fastest way to keep score on the court.

Track every point right from your wrist.
Built for casual and rec play, with Live Activity and voice.

LIVE ACTIVITY + DYNAMIC ISLAND

Score on your Lock Screen — and your iPhone notch.

When the game starts, PickleBoard pins the score to your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island. No app to open. No interruption to play.

VOICE ANNOUNCEMENT

Hear every score. “4, 2, 1.”

After each point, your Watch announces the score aloud — server-receiver-server-number, the way the pros call it. Your eyes stay on the ball.

MATCH STATS, WEATHER & SHARING

Remember every match.

When the game ends, PickleBoard captures the score, weather, and location of your match. Tap Rematch to start the next one. Share a card to brag about it.

SCORING MODES

Doubles, singles, or rally — your call.

Doubles

Standard pickleball doubles with automatic server rotation and side-out tracking. Default mode.

Singles

Singles serving rules with proper 1v1 server rotation. Switch in Settings.

Rally

Score every rally, not just your serve. Optional freeze-at-target for tight finishes. Targets of 11, 15, or 21 with automatic win-by-2.
