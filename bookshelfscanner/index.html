Features

Everything you need to run your library

From scanning books to tracking checkouts, Bookshelf Scanner handles it all.

📷

Instant Barcode Scanning

Point your camera at any book's ISBN barcode to add it to your library in seconds. Title, author, and cover art are fetched automatically.

👩‍🏫

Student Checkout Tracking

Lend books to students and track who has what. Set due dates, record returns, and keep a full checkout history.

📚

Library Organization

Organize books by status, location, and shelf. Create custom statuses and locations that match your classroom setup.

Custom Statuses Locations Shelves
📤

Export to CSV & PDF

Export your book catalog or student roster as spreadsheets or printable reports. Share with colleagues or keep for your records.

💾

Backup & Restore

Create full library backups saved to iCloud Drive. Restore your entire library on a new device with one tap.

🔒

Private & Ad-Free

No ads, no tracking, no analytics. Your library data stays on your device and in your iCloud account. Student data is never shared.

☁️

iCloud Sync

Your library syncs automatically across all your Apple devices. Add a book on your phone, check it out on your iPad.

💻

Mac Support

Manage your library from your Mac with webcam barcode scanning. Same great app, now on your desktop.

📋

Bulk Student Import

Import your class roster from a CSV file. No need to type each student name individually.

How It Works

Up and running in three steps

1

Scan Your Books

Point your camera at each book's barcode. Bookshelf Scanner looks up the title, author, and cover automatically.

2

Add Your Students

Type student names or import a roster from a CSV file. Assign borrower codes for quick checkout.

3

Start Lending

Check out books to students with a tap. Track due dates, record returns, and see your full lending history.
